Your Ventilation

Did you know that improper attic ventilation not only inflates your electric bill in the summer, it can also cut years off the life of your shingle roof?

Here in North Texas the heat can build up in your attic, and not only increase the amount of electricity needed to cool your house, it also stresses your AC unit, and weakens your shingles. If you want to cut your over sized electric bill, extend the life of your AC unit, and keep your shingles from melting off the roof, give us a call. We’ll review your attic ventilation and give you an optimized solution to protect your home and family.

About Us

Owner and president, Jeffrey L Schild, has been installing commercial and residential roofs since 2007. He worked for five years in the Washington DC/Northern VA/Maryland area installing many commercial roofs. Jeff has experience installing all of the various kinds of roofs including standing seam metal roofs, green roofs, hot rubberized asphalt roofs, single ply membrane roofing (includes EPDM, TPO and PVC) and any kind of coping or flashing work. In 2012, Jeff was the operations manager for a small roofing company in Jacksonville, Florida, that installed over 300 roofs in nine months. He was responsible for managing three installation crews, ordering materials, scheduling inspections and customer satisfaction. During his time as the operations manager, the roofing company had an A+ rating with the BBB. As a degreed electrical engineer, Jeff makes sure all his installations are in full compliance with current International Residential Building Codes. Most insurance companies miss many code upgrade items in their scopes to clients. Through Jeff’s expertise in the building codes, clients receive the maximum amount due them for roof replacement claims.

Schild Roofing, Inc. has kept an A+ rating with the North Dallas Better Business Bureau since starting in 2012.
